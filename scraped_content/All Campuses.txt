Our Campus Network
Our Campus Network
Experience a world class  global education
For more than a century  Northeastern has been a recognized leader in experience driven learning  creating a distinctive approach to teaching and research  Over the past decade  we ve launched 13 campuses across the U S   Canada  and the U K  Each Northeastern location offers varying combinations of undergraduate  graduate  and professional learning  use inspired research  and entrepreneurship all aligned with the talent and innovation needs of the region
At each of the following campuses  you ll find carefully curated graduate programs designed to help you excel in each region s most in demand fields
Arlington
Our Arlington  Virginia campus is a hub of technological innovation  security  defense  and policy  Our graduate programs in areas such as security and resilience studies  information systems  and more  prepare learners to lead in the Washington DCR jobs of the future
Learn more about our Arlington campus
Boston
Northeastern s New England flagship campus builds on Boston s legacy of innovation and higher education  You ll find opportunities for experiential learning  research  entrepreneurship  recreation  and residential life and tackle some of the world s most pressing problems alongside expert faculty in our more than 30 federally funded research centers and institutes
Learn more about the Boston campus
Charlotte
Home to Northeastern s Center for Health Sciences  our Charlotte campus offers academic programming focused on life sciences and relevant to the region s growing health sector  Our extensive network of industry partners provides students unparalleled opportunities to develop in demand skills in a real world environment
Learn more about the Charlotte campus
London
Northeastern University London offers a vibrant community with a bespoke student experience  You ll find programs spanning the humanities  business  and data and computer science  as well as a variety of degree formats all designed to equip you with the skills and knowledge most desired by employers  organizations  and society
Learn more about Northeastern University London
Miami
Our Miami campus is in Wynwood  the city s creative and cultural hub  Access unique academic programming designed to help you gain the credentials and skills you need to excel in the region s expanding tech  finance  and health sectors  no matter your academic background
Learn more about our Miami campus
Oakland
Northeastern s flagship west coast campus the result of a merger with Mills College  offers opportunities for learning  recreation  entrepreneurship  and research  Through industry aligned graduate programs in areas like game design  biotechnology  computer science  you ll become an agile problem solver and expand your connections in the Bay Area s vibrant innovation ecosystem of startups  scaled tech  and social impact
Learn more about our Oakland campus
Portland
Northeastern s Roux Institute  on our Portland  Maine campus  offers graduate programming designed around Maine s urgent talent needs in the advanced life sciences and technology  You ll pursue real world experience through co ops and special projects with the institute s employer partners  including MaineHealth  L L  Bean  Wex  and others
Learn more about the Portland  Maine  campus
San Francisco
Our San Francisco campus is at the center of the city s exciting ecosystem of start ups and global companies  Explore programs in areas such as computing and engineering and learn to become a creative and entrepreneurial leader  no matter where your career takes you
Learn more about the San Francisco campus
Silicon Valley
Neighbor to some of the world s largest technology companies including Apple  Cisco  Google  HP  Intel  and Oracle  our Silicon Valley campus in downtown San Jose prepares you to thrive in an ever changing tech driven ecosystem and serves as a bridge to the region s corporate and startup communities
Learn more about the Silicon Valley campus
Seattle
Our Seattle campus is in the South Lake Union neighborhood  an epicenter for innovation and discovery and home to tech titans such as Amazon  Facebook  and Google  Our degree programs prepare you for careers in the rapidly expanding fields of technology  data analytics  and more  And our campus is a place where thought leaders come together to learn  network  and inspire new technologies and research
Learn more about the Seattle campus
Toronto
At our Toronto campus  you can access a world class education tailored to the economic development needs of Canada s high tech hub  Flexible degree programs in areas such as informatics  analytics  project management  and regulatory affairs prepare you for roles in fast growing fields
Learn more about the Toronto campus
Vancouver
Located in the international superhub known as the Cascadia Innovation Corridor  our Vancouver campus prepares you to join British Columbia s fast growing tech and innovation ecosystem  Programs such as data analytics engineering  computer science  and information design will help you advance in or change careers
Learn more about the Vancouver campus
