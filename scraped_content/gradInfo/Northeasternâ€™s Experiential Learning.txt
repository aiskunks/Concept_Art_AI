We Made Our Mark in Experiential Learning
Our signature experiential learning model combines academic scholarship and professional practice  Students learn to put ideas into action  And they graduate with firsthand industry knowledge
Now Make Your Mark in the Field of Your Choice
Gain skills and knowledge essential for tomorrow s careers  Or get ahead in the one you have now  Choose the option that suits your lifestyle and ambitions
Full Time Co op Opportunities  Forty percent of our master s degree programs offer full time co op opportunities  Students pursue paid positions in their field of study for three to six months
Experiential Network  XN   Students work virtually with a supervisor at a sponsoring business or nonprofit  completing a challenging assignment over a six week period
Experiential Learning at Work  Working professionals collaborate with their employer to identify a project that addresses a key business need  They also gain invaluable experience in an area that interests them
Experiential PhD   Northeastern is pioneering a new form of doctorate one that expands lab and classroom learning with paid fellowships and internships at companies  research institutes  and nonprofits  Instead of one faculty mentor  students have a second in industry  While expanding their career network  they gain access to leading innovators  publishing opportunities  and exciting career paths
Research  Lab based courses provide students with hands on experience  helping them take their research  and career  to the next level
In Class Case Studies   From case studies and exercises  students apply a real world perspective to all they learn
Take the Next Step
At Northeastern  students acquire practical skills and strategies for professional advancement  And they access a network that includes more than 3 100 employers in 136 countries
Experience is a powerful way to learn  Our graduates acquire perspectives and skills that advance their careers for life in a format  and on a schedule  that fits their lifestyle
