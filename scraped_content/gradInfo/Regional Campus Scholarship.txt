Regional Campus Scholarship
Today s employers need top talent to fill critical positions in the Technology  Life Sciences  and related industries  Companies are seeking individuals with strong capabilities in analytics  information technology  biotechnology  cybersecurity  regulatory affairs  and project management  To address this need  Northeastern University is pleased to announce new scholarships to help prepare students for these high growth industries aligned with several of our regional campuses

Student Eligibility
Select merit based scholarships of up to 25  off total program cost are available to new domestic students who enroll and start in an eligible program  Program admissions eligibility applies  and scholarship will be applied to the student account during each term of enrollment  Multiple start dates are available by program and can be accessed by clicking on the program links provided by campus below
Eligibility for scholarships at the Charlotte or Silicon Valley campus are limited to U S  residents only  Eligibility for scholarships at the Toronto campus is limited to Canadian citizens and permanent residents  Northeastern alumni are not eligible for these specific scholarships  but are encouraged to apply for the Double Husky Scholarship as appropriate
The College of Engineering at Northeastern University offers graduate students a variety of means of financial assistance  For more information  please click here
How to Apply
In 250 words or less  describe a challenge you are facing and how a Northeastern University degree will help you overcome this to become positioned for success
Submit essay to the program s respective faculty member via email as listed by campus below  Applications are reviewed upon receipt and funding is limited so students are encouraged to apply at least two weeks prior to desired start term
