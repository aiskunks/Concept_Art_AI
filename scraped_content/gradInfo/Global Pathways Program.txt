Global Pathways Program
Global Pathways Program
Global Pathways is an intensive academic program that helps students improve their English language skills while becoming comfortable with the culture of an American university  You will also earn early academic credit toward your degree program
This program is carefully designed to offer dedicated support in a safe  welcoming environment  It fully prepares you to succeed in a graduate level program from a top tier research university in the U S
Global Pathways is intended for international students who qualify academically  however do not meet Northeastern s English language admission requirements for their desired graduate degree program  This program is taken for one or two semesters before admission to their program of interest
Upon successful completion of the program requirements  you will be admitted to your graduate degree program of choice
