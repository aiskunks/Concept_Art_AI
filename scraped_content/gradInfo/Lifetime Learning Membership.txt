Lifetime Learning Membership
Unique Northeastern opportunities for students to create their own learning journeys and make the most out of the Northeastern network
By 2022  18 percent of jobs will require a master s degree  according to the Bureau of Labor Statistics and advanced degree holders earn a salary an average 35 percent higher than bachelor s degree holders  As a member of the Northeastern network  all students benefit from opportunities to pursue graduate studies in a variety of ways that speak to their interests and motivations
As a member of the Northeastern community  you are already a member of the Lifetime Learning Network  Your Lifetime Learning Membership provides you with post graduate degree opportunities and pathways to nearly 200 master s and doctoral degrees  bootcamps  and certificate programs across the globe  Learn on Northeastern s campuses in Charlotte  Seattle  San Francisco Bay Area  Toronto  as well as in Boston and online  Benefits include programs to maximize your investment  fast track your enrollment  and make the most of your Northeastern network  So whether you re pursuing an undergraduate education or returning to earn your next credential  your Lifetime Learning Membership is here to shape and extend your Northeastern experience  Learn more about these benefits below  and take advantage of these opportunities by contacting a Northeastern University Admissions Representative at 617 373 2400  or by emailing  email protected

Pathways to Earning a Graduate Credential
Creating Your Northeastern Learning Journey
The Northeastern Edge Program
The Northeastern Edge Program allows you to earn a second credential during your Northeastern experience  You complete your bachelor s in four years and use your fifth year to begin your graduate studies and add more experiential opportunities  Alternatively  if you have completed your bachelor s degree in four years  you may return  within five years  to complete your next credential
Learn more about the NU Edge Program
PlusOne Programs
If you know your desired professional path and are considering options in your degree discipline or college  then a PlusOne Accelerated Master s Program may be a good fit  These programs combine your graduate studies with your undergraduate studies  within a specific discipline or college  This allows you to earn your master s in your chosen field while you complete your bachelor s degree
Accelerating Your Entry Fast Track Your Enrollment
The Accelerated Entry Program
Your success in your undergraduate experience positions you to take advantage of fast track enrollment into a professional master s program  a bootcamp  or a certificate program  Northeastern offers over 200 eligible accelerated entry programs  Just meet the required criteria for guaranteed admission
Learn more about accelerating your entry
Experience the Privilege of Being a Double Husky
Become a Double Husky
Pursuing post baccalaureate pathways makes you a Double Husky  and with that comes an opportunity to maximize your investment  The Double Husky Scholarship is a 25  discount on tuition for eligible graduate programs
Learn more about the Double Husky
Parents and Siblings Benefit
The Parent and Family Scholarship
As an undergraduate  your network is important to us  Your parents and siblings are eligible for our Parent and Family Scholarship
Learn more about the Parent and Family Scholarship
