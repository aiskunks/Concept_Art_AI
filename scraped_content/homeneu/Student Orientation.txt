Husky 101 Student and Family Orientation
Husky 101 Student and Family Orientation gives you a taste of your new life as a Husky  This mandatory program for newly enrolled first year  transfer  and veteran undergraduate students introduces you and your family to the university  as well as the campus resources and services that will support your transition into the Northeastern community  Husky 101 will connect you with your classmates and academic advising team  engage you in student clubs and organizations  and provide you the platforms to begin charting your Northeastern path with global  research  and experiential opportunities and programs
Fall 2022 Entry
In mid April  newly enrolled undergraduate students and families will receive Husky Happenings email communications from the Office of Student Orientation and Family Programs with critical information and next steps in the Husky 101 Orientation process
