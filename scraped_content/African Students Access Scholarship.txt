African Students Access Scholarship
African Students Access Scholarship
As part of the Africa Global Initiative  Northeastern University has instituted the African Students Access Scholarship for academically outstanding students  The scholarship offers a tuition scholarship to meritorious students from African universities who have been admitted into a graduate program
Who is eligible
Incoming graduate students to one of Northeastern s U S  campuses are eligible to apply for the scholarship  Students currently enrolled at Northeastern University are not eligible
Which academic programs are eligible for the African Students Access Scholarship
The scholarship applies to a diverse mix of graduate programs  Please view the list of eligible programs at the bottom of this page
How much is the scholarship
The total scholarship amount will depend on the program you re interested in  but you may receive a 50  tuition scholarship for the entire duration of your program  provided you are in good academic standing  If you have other scholarships or education awards from other organizations  you can apply those to your program cost as well
What is the application process for the scholarship
There is no separate application required for the scholarship  You must apply to one of the eligible graduate programs  If you re admitted  you will automatically be considered for the scholarship
Find your area of interest before you start your Northeastern learning journey
All programs that meet F 1 visa requirements with on ground classes at any of our U S  campuses are eligible for this scholarship
